About CareMate

CareMate is a multilingual AI-powered chatbot designed to make healthcare awareness accessible to everyone. Users can describe their symptoms in their native language and receive responsible guidance.

It uses AI and NLP to detect symptoms and provide early advice. While not a substitute for doctors, it helps in self-care and understanding possible health concerns.

Future features include voice input, regional health data integration, and expanding the symptom database for better coverage.
